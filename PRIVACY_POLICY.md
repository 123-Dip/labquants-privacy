# Privacy Policy for LabQuants

**Last updated:** 2026-02-08

This Privacy Policy describes how LabQuants ("we", "us", or "our") handles information when you use our mobile application ("App").

## 1. Information Collection and Use

**We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal identifiable information (PII) from our users.**

Our App functions as a standalone calculator and utility tool. All data you enter into the calculators (such as chemical measurements, weight, dosage, etc.) is processed entirely locally on your device in real-time. We do not have access to this data, and it is never sent to any external server.

## 2. Local Storage and Data Persistence

The App uses your device's local storage capabilities for the following limited purposes:

*   **App Preferences:** We use `SharedPreferences` (on Android/iOS) to save your app configuration settings, such as your preferred color theme or default calculator settings. This data remains on your device and is not shared with us or any third parties.
*   **File Generation:** The App generates PDF reports based on your inputs. These files are created locally on your device. You have full control over these files and can choose to save, share, or delete them using your device's standard file management capabilities.

## 3. Permissions

The App may request the following permissions to function correctly:

*   **Storage / Photos / Media:** Required only to save or share the generated PDF reports to your device's storage.

## 4. Third-Party Services

Our App serves as a calculator and does not integrate with third-party analytics services (like Google Analytics or Firebase) or advertising networks. We do not track your usage or behavior within the App.

## 5. Security

Since we do not collect or transmit your data to any server, there is no risk of your data being intercepted or breached from our end. Your data resides solely on your device, and its security depends on your device's security settings.

## 6. Children's Privacy

Our App does not address anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal identifiable information from children under 13.

## 7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Thus, you are advised to review this page periodically for any changes. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.
